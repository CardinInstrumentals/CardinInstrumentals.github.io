Cardin Instrumentals

Shaun Cardin: Who am I and what do I do?

My name is Shaun Cardin; I make instrumentals using Fruity Loop Studio 12. I produce these instrumentals to give artists something to sing or rap to. I produce instrumentals that cost money and I encourage you to buy them, the ones I charge for are my best tracks; I highly appreciate everyone who has listened to my projects. Something else I do is post to my YouTube channel called Cardin Instrumentals, all of my free projects are available on my channel with possible previews of what is to come.

Music is a passion of mine and I hope that I could continue this passion with your support. I am still learning new things everyday and my instrumentals are gradually getting better. I would be grateful if you could listen to a few of my instrumentals and could tell me how I can potentially improve. I hope to develop this trademark I have created: Cardin Instrumentals. This trademark represents me and I hope that one day I can develop this brand into a lifestyle. Make sure to let me know if you have bought any of my projects or if you have listened to them so that I can personally contact you. Thank you for taking the time to read this.

Production: How do I make my instrumentals?

All of my projects are made using fruity loops studio 12. I have been using this software for over a year now and I have developed many skills along the way. I use a range of techniques in order to get my music to top quality and suitable for artists to use. All the music on this website was created by me personally, if you do ever want a custom track made or want me to make a track inspired by somebody, contact me. All of the music on this website is royalty and copyright free.


If you have any more questions regarding the production, make sure to contact me so I can answer any queries. Do remember that even though I have projects that you have to buy; I also have free projects that you might want to check out. If so, click on the 'Free Projects' button on the navigation. Enjoy!
