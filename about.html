Shaun Cardin: Who am I and what do I do?

My name is Shaun Cardin; I make instrumentals using a variety of software. I produce these instrumentals to help musicians as they can get free instrumentals. I do produce instrumentals that cost money and I encourage you to buy them; I highly appreciate everyone who has listened to my projects. Something else I do is post to my YouTube channel called Cardin Instrumentals - I hope you subscribe because I work hard creating instrumentals on a weekly basis.

Music is a passion of mine and I hope that I could continue this passion with your support. I am still learning new things everyday and my instrumentals are gradually getting better. I would be grateful if you could listen to a few of my instrumentals and could tell me how I can potentially improve. I hope to develop this trademark I have created: Cardin Instrumentals. This trademark represents me and I hope that one day I can develop this brand into a lifestyle. Make sure to let me know if you have bought any of my projects or if you have listened to them so that I can personally contact you. Thank you for taking the time to read this.

Prices of Projects: How much are you going to charge?

Most of my projects are going to be at cheap prices. As of now, the quality of my instrumentals would most likely be targeted at a price of £2 or under. When I progressively get better, I would be expecting to price my projects at around £5 or under. The projects I create are deliberately affordable so that it is convenient for buyers and I can also produce alot of volume at low prices.


If you have any more questions regarding the pricing, make sure to contact me so I can answer any queries. Even though I have projects that you have to buy; I also have free projects that you might want to check out. If so, click on the 'Free Projects' button on the navigation. Enjoy!
